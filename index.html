About Me

Most businesses today are surrounded by data but still struggle to answer simple questions like "What's working?" and "What should we do next?"

That gap between data and decision-making is exactly where I bring value. I help businesses and teams turn raw, unstructured data into clear, actionable insights that support smarter decisions — without getting lost in complexity or overwhelming dashboards.

My journey into business analytics started with curiosity about understanding the "why" behind numbers. While working on projects like consumer behavior analysis in the bubble tea industry and building performance dashboards, I realized that data is only valuable when it tells a story that businesses can act on.

I'm passionate about simplifying data, identifying patterns, and translating insights into meaningful business outcomes — whether it's improving performance, understanding customer behavior, or uncovering hidden opportunities.

As a Business Analyst, I am continuously building my skills in Advanced Excel, SQL, Python, Power BI, and data visualization to solve real-world business problems and create impact through data-driven decision-making.
